Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the challenge

What are the prize values?

First place receives $2.5M in equity, second place $1.5M, and third place $1M, based on planned Wefunder Community Raise campaign and company valuation target of $250M in April of 2026. The company reserves the right to modify the value of the equity prizes based on the actual valuation established.

How does Astra Intelligence scoring work?

Astra automatically tracks how your team uses AI Rocket throughout the challenge, assigning weighted scores across Run, Build, and Grow dimensions. These scores remain hidden until the challenge ends and represent 50% of your final evaluation score.

What is the Impact Statement?

The Impact Statement is a required part of your Final Application where you answer: "What positive impact are you able to make in the world with your new AI-Powered business?" This demonstrates alignment with our core mission and constitutes 25% of your challenge score.

How is the final score calculated?

Your final score combines three elements: Impact Statement (25%) demonstrating the positive impact you'll make in the world, RBG written summaries (25%) showing your transformation across Run-Build-Grow, and Astra Intelligence automated tracking (50%) measuring your actual AI Rocket usage. Additional factors include social media engagement and bonus points for team invitations.

How many team members can participate?

Each team can include up to 3 members: 1 primary user and up to 2 additional invited team members. All team members get full unlimited access during the challenge.

Are there bonus points available?

Yes, teams can earn bonus points through exceptional social media engagement and by inviting other eligible teams to join the challenge (until the 300-team capacity is reached).

What happens if I don't win a top 3 spot?

All teams that submit valid prize applications during the March 16-20 window receive a 1-year unlimited subscription to AI Rocket‚Äîregardless of final placement!

Can I opt out of the challenge?

Yes, you can opt-out at any time. However, opting out forfeits your unlimited subscription benefit. We hope you'll stay and participate!

How are winners selected?

Winners are selected by the AI Rocket Advisory Committee based on the scoring breakdown: Impact Statement demonstrating positive world impact (25%), RBG written summaries (25%), Astra Intelligence automated usage scores (50%), plus social media engagement and bonus points.

Which mastermind groups are eligible?

Members of Gobundance, EO, YPO, Strategic Coach, Genius Network, R360, and similar entrepreneur mastermind groups are eligible.

How does AI Rocket handle my data privacy and security?

AI Rocket syncs with your selected data only after you give authorization, and only with selected data directed by you. The data sync is fully encrypted to the highest industry standards. AI Rocket will not train on or use any user data without express written consent.

When does registration open?

Early-Access signup begins in mid-December 2025 for invited entrepreneurs. The Challenge officially launches January 1, 2026. Entry is limited to the first 300 teams who Launch their AI Rocket.

What happens after I register?

You'll immediately receive unlimited access to AI Rocket for 90 days. Start using the platform to transform your business across Run, Build, and Grow dimensions while Astra Intelligence tracks your progress.
